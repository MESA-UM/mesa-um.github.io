About us

Malaya Electrical Students Association (MESA) is an established society under Department of Electrical Engineering, University of Malaya founded in 2018. It is the official association created for students studying in the discipline of Electrical Engineering in University of Malaya for coordination of intra and extramural student activities within the faculty.

Our Major Events

Industrial Talks/Visit

Industrial Talks/Visits held throughout both semester 1 and 2 with the goal of giving EE students exposure to the latest developments of the industry 

MESA Orientation

Annual orientation program organized by MESA with the objective of helping 1st year UMEE students to understand their department,faculty,university better while also having the opportunity to know their seniors 

Industrial Open Day

An annual flagship event by MESA with the main objective of not only providing students exposure to the industry, but also to assist third and final year students in securing internship opportunities and permanent job placement.
