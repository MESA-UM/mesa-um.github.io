ABOUT US

WHY MESA?

We aim to accelerate student's personal and professional development through exposure and network while serving as a hub to connect the bridge between the department and students. By doing so, we envision to prepare the students to be more workforce ready.

WHO WE ARE?

Establishment in 2018, MESA is a student-run association that is registered under the Department of Electrical Engineering, UM. MESA operates under the supervision of two Student Advisors from the department, which are Dr. Wong Wei Ru & Dr. Sharifah.

WHAT DO WE DO?

We organize industrial talks and visits to equip and empower the students with industry-ready skills and knowledge. We also host some personal skills development talks to develop the students in terms of their soft skills and personality
